About

Rod Snyder is a technologist and writer exploring the frontier of artificial intelligence. With a background spanning decades in technology and a prolific career in journalism, he brings a unique perspective to AI research and development — combining technical depth with the storyteller's instinct for clarity.

Currently, Rod is building full-stack web applications with AI-assisted development tools, researching the practical applications of large language models, and documenting the journey. His work sits at the intersection of technology and human experience.

A competitive Masters Olympic weightlifter and active community member, Rod brings the same discipline and persistence to his technical work that defines his approach to everything he does.
